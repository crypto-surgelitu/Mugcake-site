How to make chocolate mugcake

This quick chocolate mug cake is ready in just 90 seconds — rich, gooey, and perfect for sudden dessert cravings.

Ingredients

  1. 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  2. 4 tablespoons sugar
  3. 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  4. 1/8 teaspoon baking powder
  5. 3 tablespoons milk
  6. 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  7. 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  8. Optional: chocolate chips, nuts, or a scoop of ice cream for topping

Instructions

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, mix the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking powder.
  2. Add milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract. Stir until smooth.
  3. Optional: add chocolate chips or nuts.
  4. Microwave for 70–90 seconds.
  5. Let cool for 1–2 minutes and enjoy.
